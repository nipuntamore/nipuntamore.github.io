The Lords of the Iron Coast

Key Characteristics of Maharaj's Sea Forts: Lead Foundations: For forts like Sindhudurg, molten lead was poured into the foundation stones to ensure they would not be eroded by the salt water. Sweet Water Wells: Despite being in the middle of the salty ocean, most of these forts (especially Sindhudurg) have wells that provide fresh drinking water. Hidden Entrances: The main gates (Mahadarwaja) are usually hidden between two bastions so they cannot be seen or targeted by enemy ships from a distance.
